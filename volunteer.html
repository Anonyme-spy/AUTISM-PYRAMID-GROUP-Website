Volunteers

Our group relies on volunteers to help us to hold Stay & Play Sessions and to support and represent us at Community and Fundraising events.

If you can spare just a few hours a month to get involved contact us: autismpyramidgroup@gmail.com

Role Description

You are required to do the following duties:

1. Help to set up furniture and equipment for Stay and Play Sessions.

2. Work alongside children and family members with their chosen activities.

3. Help children and families to access Refreshments.

4. Put away furniture and equipment at the end of the Session and assist with any necessary cleaning.

5. Provide support and assistance to families for events and outings during the school holidays.

6. Assist with any fundraising or publicity events that the Group may be attending.

7. Any other duties assigned to you by the Play Leaders.
